Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

What should I bring for a dirt biking tour?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, closed-toe shoes, and a water bottle. We provide all necessary gear, including helmets and gloves.

Are the tours suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer custom adventures tailored to all skill levels, with beginner-friendly trails and thorough safety briefings.

What happens if it rains during the tour?

Our tours run rain or shine, as long as conditions are safe. If the weather poses a risk, we’ll reschedule or provide a full refund.
