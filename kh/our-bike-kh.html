Our Bike & Gear

Honda XR 250R

Included in your trip price

The Honda XR 250R is well-suited for our standard tour packages. Its robust construction, lightweight design, and reliable performance make it ideal for riders of all skill levels. The Honda XR 250R’s versatility allows it to excel on paved roads and light off-road terrain. Its durable frame, powerful engine, and responsive handling instill confidence in both experienced and novice riders. The Honda XR 250R is a remarkable all-around motorcycle that consistently delivers exceptional performance and dependability on our tours.

Yamaha WR 250F

Available at a surcharge

The Yamaha WR 250F is an excellent choice for the experienced enduro rider seeking a highly capable and versatile off-road motorcycle. Its quick-revving engine provides impressive low and midrange power, giving riders the responsive performance needed to conquer a wide range of challenging terrain. Whether navigating technical trails, traversing steep inclines, or powering through muddy conditions, the Yamaha WR 250F is more than ready to rise to the occasion.

KTM 250 EXC

Available at a surcharge

Get ready for an exciting dirt bike adventure on the KTM 250 EXC, widely known for its 250-like agility and 450-like power. Thanks to its exceptional rideability and competitiveness on various terrains, this enduro motorcycle has gained recognition in the Enduro World Championships. Prepare yourself and your dirt bike like a pro!
