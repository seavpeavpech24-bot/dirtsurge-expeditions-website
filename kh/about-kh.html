About us

A Local Expert & Off-Road Enthusiast

Welcome to Dirtbike Expeditions, where adventure meets the untamed spirit of Cambodia! Founded by a local expert, we specialize in immersive dirt bike tours that take you deep into the heart of this beautiful and diverse country. Our passion is to share the thrill of off-road riding while showcasing the breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture of Cambodia and beyond.

At Dirtbike Expeditions, we are a dedicated team of riding enthusiasts and local guides with a deep love for our homeland. We have spent years exploring Cambodia’s remote trails and hidden gems. Our extensive knowledge of the terrain and its history ensures that every expedition is not just an adventure but also an opportunity to connect with the culture and stories of the land.

Our Story

At DirtSurge Expedition, we’re more than just guides—we’re riders at heart. Founded by locals with a passion for off-road biking, our mission is to bring you thrilling, safe, and unforgettable rides through the wild trails of Cambodia. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, we’ve got an adventure waiting for you.
